# Functions for formula manipulation

Functions for formula manipulation

## Details

- [`combine_formulas`](https://statisticsnorway.github.io/ssb-ssbtools/reference/combine_formulas.md):
  Combine formulas

- [`formula_from_vars`](https://statisticsnorway.github.io/ssb-ssbtools/reference/formula_from_vars.md):
  Generate model formula by specifying which variables have totals or
  not

- [`substitute_formula_vars`](https://statisticsnorway.github.io/ssb-ssbtools/reference/substitute_formula_vars.md):
  Replace variables in formula with sum of other variables

- [`formula_term_labels`](https://statisticsnorway.github.io/ssb-ssbtools/reference/formula_term_labels.md):
  Retrieve term labels from a formula
